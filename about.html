About S.K.INDUSTRIES

S.K.INDUSTRIES., is one of the leading company into manufacturing of multi Operational precision turn components.
S.K.INDUSTRIES., has been catering to most of the OEM’s, MNC’s & Pvt. in the AUTOMOTIVE & its Allied Sectors.
S.K.INDUSTRIES., assures incorporated systems for sustaining a good quality and excellence in manufacturing.
S.K.INDUSTRIES., is into manufacturing of precision machined components and assemblies with varying raw materials.

S.K.INDUSTRIES., defines its performance based on the following factors: *Quality
*Cost

S.K.INDUSTRIES. adapts all the required quality measures & standards to maintained higher productivity with efficient use of Man, Machine & Material.
